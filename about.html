About Gairah Resort

Welcome to the Most Homely Resort in Urumbikkara

Gairah Resorts has been conceived with making it the finest off-beat off-road resort in Urumbikkara, providing comfortable accommodation and amenities to the adventure traveller, the wellness seeker and the nature lover, all quietly underlined with our commitment to superior customer care & service. We are located 75 minutes from the the Kottayam Rail terminus and 19 kms away from the Mundakkayam Bus Station, with the resort nestled in a gentle slope on the Vellappara-Peruvanthanam Road. In Summer, it is the perfect vacation destination in taking the heat off with exploring the magnificent attractions in Urumbikkara, and with the onset of Kerala’s Monsoon, the magnificence transforms to sheer jaw-dropping natural verdant grandeur of Urumbikkara that has to be seen and experienced.

The Concept of Gairah

Gairah, with its root usages in Malay translates to passion, zest, spirit, warmth, peacefulness – which broadly reflects the singular objective of this homely destination in wild travels. The logo encapsulates all that we believe this stands for in the context of God’s own Magnificent country, with an undulating illustrative that depicts the roads one takes or makes to follow one’s passion, also subliminally bringing out our favorite symbol of magnificience in the wild – the Kerala Tusker.

The perfect blend of Adventure and Comfort

Consider Gairah Resorts to be the base of your wondrous adventure to the untouched tropical delight that is Urumbikkara. You could begin with a super-short trek to Pappanithodu falls – a hidden jewel of Western Ghats with a 600 feet sheer drop, or if feeling a little more wild, to the Wembly waterfalls or Vellappara waterfalls – both 2.5 km away from the resort. And that is just the beginning.

Take a Walk into the Wild

We could plan a day-trip for you Urumbikkara‘s other favorite attractions too. If you would rather stay indoors, you have the best of comfort from our thoughtfuly created Cottages, or lose yourself in the finest choice of cuisine from Eatopia, our dining nook at Gairah Resorts.
